How We Can Help You

Unexpected injury always comes as a shock. In addition to the physical pain that comes with personal injury, you suddenly have a slew of new problems. How can I pay these medical bills? Will I need to take time off of work? How will I recover? Do I need to get a lawyer? The misconception is that it's just a physical injury, but it can take a toll financially and mentally, as well.
It can be an overwhelming time in your life, which is why is why you owe it to yourself to make it as painless as possible. In a chaotic time, the right lawyer can make all the difference.
Your have one job: to recover from your injuries. We'll deal with everything else for you.

About Us

In life, the one thing you can't teach is experience. Mr. Nolan has been practicing and enforcing the law for close to 40 years. He has been practicing personal injury law for 27 years since graduating from Suffolk Law School in 1992. In addition to his law career, Mr. Nolan served as a member of the Milton Police Department for nearly 30 years, retiring as the Deputy Police Chief in 2010 due to injuries sustained in the line of duty.
